Ingredients:
 - 2 slices of whole wheat bread (should be soft)
 - 1 teaspoon of butter (our secret weapon)
 - 1 tablespoon of peanut butter (more if you are really like it)
 - 2 teaspoons of grape jelly

Directions:
 1. Spread a half teaspoon of butter on each slice of bread.

 2. Spread one slice with peanut butter.

 3. Swirl the grape jelly into the peanut butter

 4. Cover the other slice of bread.

 5. Eat and drink with a whole glass of milk. Arrival to heaven awaits.
